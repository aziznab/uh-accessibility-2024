Project Details

SnapGenAI

SnapGenAI is an innovative mobile application that seamlessly integrates camera functionality with Generative AI. It offers users the unique ability to capture images and instantly transform them into creative works of art using advanced AI algorithms.

VisualSizer

VisualSizer helps avoid the frustration of receiving an ill-fitting garment by revolutionizing the way we choose sizes for our clothes. It measures body parts like head, shoulders, and legs using Python, OpenCV, and OpenPose.

Cookix

In Cookix, you manage a busy kitchen and serve customers by fulfilling orders within a time limit. The game challenges you to work quickly and efficiently, dealing with unexpected events and complex dishes.
